General Surgery
Second Year Nursing

📌 Definition of Inflammation

Inflammation is a reaction of living tissues to injury or infection.

It occurs as a defense mechanism to remove harmful agents and start healing.

⚠️ Causes of Inflammation

• Bacteria and microorganisms
• Viruses
• Burns
• Electricity
• Chemicals
• Trauma

🦠 Types of Bacterial Inflammation

1- Acute inflammation
2- Chronic inflammation
3- Specific inflammation
4- Non-specific inflammation

🌡️ Symptoms

• Elevated body temperature
• Chills
• Sweating
• Lethargy
• Increased pulse

💊 Treatment

• Complete rest
• Elevation of affected limb
• Fluids
• Antibiotics & analgesics
• Compresses
• Drainage of pus when needed
